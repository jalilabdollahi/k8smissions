running install.sh for the first time.

  - Role "k8smissions-player-role" with full CRUD on:

      persistentvolumeclaims, ingresses, endpoints,
      statefulsets, replicasets, daemonsets, jobs, cronjobs,
      horizontalpodautoscalers, networkpolicies,
      poddisruptionbudgets, serviceaccounts, roles, rolebindings
  - RoleBinding: bind the role to the ServiceAccount

      nodes, namespaces, storageclasses, persistentvolumes,
      clusterroles, clusterrolebindings
  - ClusterRoleBinding for the viewer role

SAFETY PRINCIPLE:
  Players should NOT be able to modify:
  - kube-system namespace
  - kube-public namespace
  - Node objects (creation/deletion)
  - CRDs
  - ClusterRole / ClusterRoleBinding (write access)
