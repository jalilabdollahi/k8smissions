Both app-stable and app-canary have 5 replicas. The total is 10 pods. 5 are canary — that is 50% of traffic going to an unvalidated version.

For a proper canary, only 10% of traffic should go to canary. With a total of 10 pods, that means:
- stable: 9 pods
- canary: 1 pod

Check the Deployments:

  kubectl get deployments -n k8smissions
