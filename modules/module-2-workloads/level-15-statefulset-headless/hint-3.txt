A StatefulSet requires its `serviceName` to point to a **headless** Service — one with `clusterIP: None`. Without this, Kubernetes cannot create the stable DNS entries (`db-0.db-svc`) that StatefulSet pods depend on.

The current Service `db-svc` is a regular ClusterIP Service. You need to either modify it to be headless or create a new headless Service and update the StatefulSet's serviceName.
