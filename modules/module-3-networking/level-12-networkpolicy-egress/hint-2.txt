The NetworkPolicy selects all pods (`podSelector: {}`) and has policyType `Egress` with an empty `egress: []` list. In Kubernetes, when policyTypes includes Egress and egress rules are empty, ALL outbound traffic is blocked — including DNS queries to port 53.

CoreDNS listens on UDP and TCP port 53 in the kube-system namespace. Without an egress rule allowing this, pods cannot resolve any name.
