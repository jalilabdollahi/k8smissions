WORLD 12 — Production War Games
════════════════════════════════

OVERVIEW
────────
World 12 covers multi-failure incident response, chaos engineering drills, and
production war games. Each level simulates a real production outage with multiple
concurrent failures. Players must triage, isolate, and fix issues under pressure
using all skills from previous worlds.

Difficulty   : Expert
XP per level : 300–500
Total XP     : 6,550
