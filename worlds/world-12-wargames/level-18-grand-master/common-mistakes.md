# Common Mistakes — Grand Master

## ❌ Parallelising fixes without understanding dependencies
Fix secrets/serviceaccounts before the deployments that depend on them.

## ❌ Losing track of what you've fixed
Use a checklist — there are 9 issues and it's easy to miss one.

## ❌ Not verifying each fix
Run 'check' after every 2-3 fixes to see your progress.
